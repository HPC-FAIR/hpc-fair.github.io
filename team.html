PI & Co-PI

Dr. Chunhua Liao (PI)

Senior Computer Scientist in the Center for Applied Scientific Computing (CASC) at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL). His research focus has been on software techniques to improve the performance and correctness of parallel programs. His research interests encompass parallel languages, especially OpenMP, optimizing compilers, runtime systems, and programming tools.

Prof. Xipeng Shen (CoPI)

Professor in Computer Science at NC State University (NCSU), ACM Distinguished member, DOE & NSF Career Award receipt. Research interest focuses on Compilers and Programming Systems, High Performance Computing, and Real-Time AI.

Dr. Murali Emani (CoPI)

Assistant Computer Scientist in the Data Sciences group with the Argonne Leadership Computing Facility (ALCF) at Argonne National Laboratory (ANL). His primary research focus lies in the intersection of systems and machine learning. Research interests include Parallel programming models, Hardware accelerators for ML/DL, High Performance Computing, Scalable Machine Learning, Runtime Systems, Performance optimization, Emerging HPC architectures, Online Adaptation.

Dr. Tristan Vanderbruggen (CoPI)

Computer Scientist in the Center for Applied Scientific Computing (CASC) at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL). His research focus spans source-to-source transformations, C/C++ source code analysis, and machine learning applied to compiler internal representations.
